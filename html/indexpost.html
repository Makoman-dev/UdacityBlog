Jax

The Grandmaster at Arms

Current Company/School: Champion at League of Legends

Jax is the last known weapons master of Icathia, a land devastated by the Void. After his homeland's destruction, he vowed to protect what remained and now roams Valoran, seeking warriors worthy of wielding the last light of Icathia. Known for his exceptional skill with his unique armaments and his biting sarcasm, Jax is a force to be reckoned with in the League of Legends.
